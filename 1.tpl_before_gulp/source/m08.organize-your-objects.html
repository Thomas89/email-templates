Organize your objects

Organize your objects in groups and sections:

Definitions:

• Sections are top-level groupings to visually organize your objects. Sections contains groups. For example, a section could be Exhibitions, Inventory, or a type of object.
• Groups are folders to place your objects in. For example, a group could be the name of an exhibition, the name of an artist, or a period.

How to create sections and groups:

1. On the top right of your account homepage, you can easily create and name new sections and groups by selecting 'New Section' or 'New Group.'
2. When you add a new object, you can quickly select a group to place your object in by clicking the right one in the available dropdown. The group has to be created first.'
3. In any group, you can select one or multiple objects and select 'Actions' to create a new group, copy or move the objects to another group, or delete from the current one.
